The Placeables Prefab's id shoud be the ItemType.itemName it comes from in the inventory.

eg. TomatoPlaceable comes from the TomatoSeed item whose ItemType.itemName is "TomatoSeed". Therefore the 
id is "TomatoSeed"
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Spawners Prefab's id should be the ItemType.itemName + "Spawner" it comes from in the inventory.

eg. TomatoPlaceSpawner comes from the TomatoSeed item whose ItemType.itemName is "TomatoSeed". Therefore the 
id is "TomatoSeedSpawner"
